Privacy Policy

How we handle your data with precision.

Last updated: April 2026 · Applies to Australian Privacy Principles under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth)

Our Approach

Data handled the same way we handle complexity

At Resolved Logic, we turn tangled problems into structured solutions. We take the same approach to your personal information: we keep it organised, secure, and minimal. We follow the Australian Privacy Principles under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

Data Collection

What information we collect

As a consultancy, we don't need a lot of data to be effective. We only collect:

  • Contact Details: Your name and email address if you reach out via our contact form.
  • Project Briefs: Any information you voluntarily provide about your business challenges.
  • Technical Data: Basic "internet breadcrumbs" like your IP address and browser type via cookies, to help us understand how people are using our site.
Client Engagements

Access to proprietary data

To build solutions that actually work, we often need to "look under the hood." This may involve accessing your existing databases, proprietary spreadsheets, legacy file structures, or third-party API keys (such as Square, Stripe, or Xero).

The "Zipped Lips" Policy. We treat this data as strictly confidential. Access is obtained solely for the purposes of:
  • Mapping existing data to new, structured databases.
  • Auditing current workflows to identify technical requirements.
  • Testing the integrity of the completed solution.

We do not store your business data longer than necessary for the build, and we never share your internal logic or trade secrets with third parties. We treat your data with the same precision and security as our own code.

Data Use

How we use your information

We use your data strictly to:

  • Respond to your enquiries.
  • Provide the bespoke digital solutions you have requested.
  • Improve the performance and logic of this website.
We don't sell your data to third-party marketers. We build systems. We don't trade in people's contact lists.
Security & Storage

We are software developers, we don't leave the gate open

We use industry-standard encryption and trusted third-party hosting (including GitHub and Cloudflare) to protect your information. While no system is 100% impenetrable, we prioritise security at the code level, not as an afterthought.

Cookies

The kind that don't leave crumbs on your desk

We use cookies to help our website remember your preferences and understand site usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer to remain entirely anonymous, though some site features may be reduced as a result.

Your Rights

Access, correction, and deletion

You can ask to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. Just send us an email. If we are not required to keep it for legal or tax reasons, it will be gone promptly.

Under the Australian Privacy Principles, you also have the right to make a complaint if you believe we have not handled your information appropriately.

Complaints

Concerns about your privacy

If you have concerns about your privacy, contact us at resolvedlogic@gmail.com. We'll take it seriously and resolve it quickly.

If you are still not satisfied after we have responded, you can contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at www.oaic.gov.au.
