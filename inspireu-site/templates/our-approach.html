Our Approach

Four guiding principles that put you at the centre of your own support journey - always.

How we work

Our approach to great support

At InspireU, we don't just provide support - we build it around you. Here are the four principles that guide every interaction, every plan, and every decision we make.

01
🎯

Person-Centred Care

Your goals, your preferences, your life. We start by listening. Really listening... and build every support plan around what matters most to you. You are the expert on your own life, and we are here to support you to live it fully. This means no assumptions, no shortcuts, and no cookie-cutter approaches. Every person is unique, and your support should reflect that.

02
💜

Trauma-Informed Practice

We understand that many people living with disability have experienced trauma, whether directly related to their disability or through other life experiences. Our entire team is trained to create environments of safety, trust, and choice. We respond with sensitivity and care, and we never re-traumatise the people we support. Understanding someone's history is how we deliver truly meaningful care.

03
🌊

Flexible & Responsive

Life changes, and so do support needs. We are built to flex with you. Whether your needs shift day to day or your goals evolve over time, we adapt quickly so your support always fits your life, not the other way around. We maintain open, honest communication so that adjustments happen smoothly and you always feel in control of your own journey.

04
🌱

Independence & Wellbeing

We don't just support you today, we work toward a future where you need us less. Our goal is to build your confidence, capabilities, and connections so you can live as independently and joyfully as possible. Every skill developed, every connection made, and every goal achieved is a step toward a fuller life. That's what inspires us.

Trauma-informed care

Why trauma-informed support matters

Trauma-informed care is not just a buzzword for InspireU - it is the bedrock of how we operate. Research consistently shows that trauma is widespread and has lasting effects on people's health, relationships, and ability to engage with services.

By training every team member in trauma-informed principles, we ensure that our support never inadvertently causes harm, and instead creates the conditions for genuine healing, growth, and thriving.

  • ✓
    Safety - physical and emotional, always
  • ✓
    Trustworthiness and transparency
  • ✓
    Peer support and community
  • ✓
    Collaboration and mutuality
  • ✓
    Empowerment, voice, and choice
  • ✓
    Cultural, historical, and gender awareness
